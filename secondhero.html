Project Details

With blockchain technology, we eliminate trust assumptions by allowing anyone to verify the authenticity of data, processes and outcomes; since our solution allows us to collect data from its genesis and hash the records onto the blockchain, the tamper-proof nature of the blockchain allows the data to remain authentic and accessible when needed. Proof of Reputation-Consensus Mechanism

As we know, data stored on the blockchain is immutable, meaning it can't be altered, transactions records are stored in form of a digital distributed ledger, this contains blocks of transactions where each block is connected to the previous block, so if a person decides to alter the records or possibly cause havoc to the system, he has to do that on several blocks containing the copies of the transactions that form the chain. In a blockchain, every transaction has to be approved by a group of network participants called either miners or validators before being accepted as part of the network to form a block. Consensus mechanisms are methods that allows transactions to be approved and validated then subsequently added to the blockchain. In a blockchain network, the consensus determines the security and performance of the overall system. There are so many consensus mechanisms that have been adopted by different blockchain technologies, these mechanisms include: Proof of Work, Proof of Stake, delegated Proof of Stake, Proof of Capacity Proof of burn, e.t.c. These different mechanisms have algorithms that enable them to function in certain manner so as to safeguard the network. The Proof of Reputation is Circular's native consensus mechanism, its approach is to permit equal participation of nodes within the network while defending the network against malicious actors and at the same time preserving true decentralization. Its concept is based on the process that different nodes participating in the network gains or loses reputation based on their behaviour in the network, take for instance, when a node behaves accordingly, it gains reputation but when it behaves inappropriately, it loses reputation; the nodes with the highest reputation values eventually becomes part of a consensus group that determines the state of the blockchain. This system depends on the reputation of nodes to secure the network; the Proof of Reputation is highly secure because network participants have to work hard over a period of time in order to gain their reputation. The Proof of Reputation consensus mechanism is considered a more viable option than others because, it is environmentally friendly and doesn't require high computational power, does not require a large amount to be staked before a node can participate in the network thereby reduces financial centralization to the barest minimum. In all, the Circular's Proof of Reputation based system is economically and environmentally friendly, it plays a vital role in the web3 space and promotes the core aspects of decentralization.

Benefits of Proof of Reputation

  • Eco-friendly and low power consumption
  • No need for expensive hardware and staking
  • Encourages good network behaviour through reputation
  • prevents centralization and malicious activities
  • Supports true decentralization and transparency

Multichain Architecture

Blockchains can either be public, private or hybrid in their Architecture, though Circular protocol is classified as a public blockchain, but It is hybrid in its network, given that the network allows the functioning of both public and private chains,the private chains cater for the enterprises because they provide cheaper solutions to industries since many of them may not necessarily want to assume liability for their data beyond the lifespan of the project. The Multichain Architecture enables the Circular blockchain to scale in three dimensions namely: by increasing, the number of shards, increasing the number of chains, and by the parallelism of execution of transaction. This feature enables it to reach higher throughput over a long time. The multichain Architecture of Circular simply means that one blockchain can mint multiple blocks and increases the number of chains and performs parallel execution of codes.

Why Multichain Architecture Matters

  • Supports both public and private blockchains(hybrid network)
  • Improves scalability with sharding and parallel execution
  • Boosts speed and throughput over time
  • Ideal for enterprise-level data management and privacy
  • Enables flexible blockchain customization per project need
