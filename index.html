Revealing Real-world Adoption Through Enterprise Adoption By The Founder

In one of the tech conferences, Gianluca De Novi explained the importance of this paradigm shift from just the crypto use case of the blockchain technology to the area where enterprises utilize the tamper-proof nature of this amazing technology to put their records on-chain, in order to make certification and validation of records easier and verifiable. Blockchain technology itself is often confused with cryptocurrencies, but its true potential lies far beyond digital coins. When enterprises or industries like healthcare adapt blockchain as an infrastructure to provide tools to certify and manage data on-chain, it opens the door to meaningful real-world applications. According to Gianluca De Novi, integrating blockchain infrastructure in critical industries ensures transparency, traceability, and accountability in the sector, which eliminates trust assumptions. Enterprise adoption acts as a catalyst, it shifts blockchain from a complex financial innovation to a foundational layer of the digital economy. While crypto still remains an important part of the ecosystem, It's the data layer not the coin - that paves the way for real-world blockchain adoption. Circular protocol is the major player in the field that is driving this real-world adoption, by building a compliant infrastructure that will serve the needs of enterprises while still maintaining true decentralization in all aspects.

About Circular

Circular protocol is a layer 1, fourth generation blockchain technology with focus on reshaping trust in the critical industries, most especially healthcare. Its aim is to leverage the power of the blockchain technology to solve real-world problems that deals with managing data and patients records, by providing a sure means to ensure that data is certified from its genesis and remains tamper-proof and accessible to the patient who technically owns the data. Circular protocol is not just another layer 1, but a layer one blockchain built not only to address the problem of the blockchain trilemma but embeds programmability and performance features in it's infrastructure. Circular, as another layer 1 has provided a grand solution to the problems encountered by other players in the field, due to the fact that it isn't just designed as a secured blockchain with an advanced consensus mechanism, it is a scalable paradigm that has advanced security without the need to sacrifice decentralization.
